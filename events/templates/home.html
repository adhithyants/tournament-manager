Website Terms of Use:

Acceptable Use:

  • Prohibited activities: Spamming, harassment, data misuse, sharing of confidential information (e.g., player medical records), and any conduct that undermines the integrity of the tournaments or the wrestling community.
  • User Responsibilities: Maintaining account security, adhering to fair play, respecting opponents and officials, and complying with all applicable rules and regulations.

Privacy Policy:

  • How user data (player information, registration details) is collected, used, and protected.

Disclaimer:

Tournament Rules & Regulations:

General Rules:

  • Eligibility criteria: Age, weight categories, residency requirements (if applicable).
  • Code of Conduct: Expected behavior for wrestlers, coaches, and spectators (e.g., sportsmanship, respect for officials, no unsportsmanlike conduct).
  • Drug Testing Policy: Information on potential drug testing procedures and consequences of violations.
  • Dispute Resolution: Procedures for addressing disputes or grievances related to tournament outcomes or officiating.

Specific Rules:

  • Weight class regulations, scoring systems, match procedures, and any specific rules related to wrestling techniques or safety.

Tournament Entry:

  • Information on entry fees (if applicable), registration deadlines, and how to register for tournaments.

Prizes & Awards:

  • Information on any prizes, awards, or recognition for tournament winners and participants.

District Wrestling Association Information:

  • Contact information for the district wrestling association, including email address, phone number, and any physical address.
  • Links to the association's official website or social media pages (if available).
