Tournament Rules & Regulations

General Rules

    To ensure fair play and safety, tournaments will have specific rules and regulations.
    These encompass eligibility criteria, including age, weight categories, and residency requirements;
    A code of conduct outlining expected behavior for wrestlers, coaches, and spectators;
    and a drug testing policy detailing potential testing procedures.

Weight Classes

    Official weight divisions and weigh-in procedures outline the specific categories in which athletes compete based on their weight, ensuring fair matchups.
    Additionally, weight allowances and restrictions determine the permissible variations in weight, including any limits or allowances for competitors during weigh-ins.

Match Procedures

    Match duration, scoring system, and official signals define the length of the competition, the method of awarding points, and the gestures used by officials to communicate rulings.
    Permitted techniques and prohibited moves specify the legal actions competitors can use and the maneuvers that are not allowed to ensure safety and fairness.
